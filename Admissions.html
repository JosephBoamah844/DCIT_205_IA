Our Admission Process

Previous page

Welcome to the University of Ghana! As you embark on your academic journey, understanding the admission process is crucial. Our Computer Science department strives to make the admission procedure seamless and accessible. To begin, visit the university's official website, where you'll find detailed information on the admission requirements, including academic qualifications, entry examinations, and any additional documentation needed for the Computer Science program.

Once you've reviewed the requirements, proceed to the online application portal. The portal provides a user-friendly interface to guide you through the application process. Ensure that you have all the necessary documents and details ready, as accurate information is essential for a successful application. Take your time to fill out the application form thoroughly, providing insights into your academic achievements and personal background.

After submitting your application, stay engaged with the application status portal. Here, you can track the progress of your application, including any requests for supplementary information or updates. The admission team works diligently to process applications, and regular check-ins on the status portal will keep you informed throughout the process.

Should you be successful in securing admission, you will receive an official offer letter. Congratulations! The offer letter will contain details about the next steps, including registration, fee payment, and orientation. Be sure to adhere to the specified deadlines and follow the outlined instructions to secure your place in the Computer Science program.

Our university is committed to supporting new students in their transition. Attend orientation sessions, explore campus resources, and connect with academic advisors who will guide you through your course selections. Embrace this exciting chapter at the University of Ghana's Computer Science department, where innovation, collaboration, and academic excellence await you. We look forward to welcoming you to our vibrant community!
