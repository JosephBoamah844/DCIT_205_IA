Contact Us

For any inquiries, the University of Ghana's Computer Science Department welcomes students to reach out through multiple channels. To establish direct contact, you can utilize the provided phone numbers: +233 501 382 035 and +233 559 145 698. Whether you have questions about academic programs, admission procedures, or general departmental information, the staff is readily available to assist. Additionally, you can communicate via email by sending your queries to dcs@ug.edu.gh. This email address is a reliable and efficient means of correspondence, allowing students to articulate their concerns and receive prompt responses. The department values open communication, and these contact options are designed to ensure that students can easily access the support and information they need during their academic journey.
