Locate Us

Nestled within the heart of the prestigious University of Ghana's Legon Campus, the Computer Science Department stands as a beacon for knowledge seekers. Situated strategically, it can be easily located opposite the Mathematics Department. As you navigate the vibrant campus, keep an eye out for this hub of technological exploration and academic excellence. The department's location places it at the center of the university's academic activities, fostering an environment where students can engage with cutting-edge technologies, collaborate with peers, and partake in the dynamic learning experiences that define the University of Ghana's commitment to shaping the future of computer science. Whether you are a newcomer to the campus or a returning student, finding the Computer Science Department becomes a seamless journey, promising a wealth of educational opportunities within its distinguished walls.
